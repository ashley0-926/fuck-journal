  <h1>About the Journal</h1>

  <div class="about-section">
    <h2>Aims & Scope</h2>
    <p><strong>F.U.C.K. (Journal of Formulal Unnecessary Commentary & Knowledge)</strong> is a wholly unnecessary, entirely unqualified, and delightfully unhinged academic-style journal dedicated to the systematic study of Formula 1 nonsense.</p>
    <p>We accept papers on any topic that can be retrofitted into an academic framework, including but not limited to:</p>
    <ul>
      <li>The metaphysical implications of DRS detection zones</li>
      <li>Comparative hagiography of team principals</li>
      <li>Ontological arguments for the existence of a next-year-wins curse</li>
      <li>Any paper that uses the word "paradigm" unironically</li>
    </ul>
  </div>

    <h2>Publication Ethics</h2>
    <p>We have none. We will publish anything that fits the vibe. Plagiarism is frowned upon only because we want to see original bad ideas, not copied ones. Fabrication of data is encouraged as long as it's funny.</p>
