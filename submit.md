<div class="page-content submit-page">
  <h1>Submit an Article</h1>

  <div class="submit-intro">
    <p>We eagerly await your groundbreaking research. To submit an article, follow the simple procedure below. We accept submissions in any format that can be rendered as Markdown.</p>
  </div>

  <div class="submit-steps">
    <div class="step">
      <div class="step-number">1</div>
      <div class="step-content">
        <h3>Create a GitHub Account</h3>
        <p>If you don't already have one, <a href="https://github.com/join" target="_blank">sign up for GitHub</a>. It's free and takes approximately the same time as a single pit stop.</p>
      </div>
    </div>

    <div class="step">
      <div class="step-number">2</div>
      <div class="step-content">
        <h3>Open a New Issue</h3>
        <p>Go to the <a href="https://github.com/ashley0-926/fuck-journal/issues/new?template=article-submission.yml" target="_blank" id="submit-link">article submission page</a> and click "Get started".</p>
      </div>
    </div>

    <div class="step">
      <div class="step-number">3</div>
      <div class="step-content">
        <h3>Fill in the Details</h3>
        <p>The issue template will guide you. You'll need:</p>
        <ul>
          <li><strong>Title</strong> — Make it sound academic. Use colons. Example: <em>"The DRS Train: A Longitudinal Study of Followability in the 2023 Season"</em></li>
          <li><strong>Authors</strong> — List your co-authors (or just yourself, we don't judge)</li>
          <li><strong>Category</strong> — Pick one of our four esteemed sections</li>
          <li><strong>Abstract</strong> — A brief summary. Sound like you know what you're talking about.</li>
          <li><strong>Body</strong> — The actual content. Use as much jargon as possible.</li>
          <li><strong>References</strong> — Cite at least three things, ideally including one you made up.</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
    </div>

    <div class="step">
      <div class="step-number">4</div>
      <div class="step-content">
        <h3>Submit</h3>
        <p>Click "Submit new issue". Your article will appear on the site after a brief review period (approximately 5-15 minutes, or whenever the site fetches the next batch of issues).</p>
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>

  <div class="submit-guidelines">
    <h2>Submission Guidelines</h2>
    <div class="guidelines-grid">
      <div class="guideline">
        <h4>✅ DO</h4>
        <ul>
          <li>Use academic language excessively</li>
          <li>Include fake statistics (be creative)</li>
          <li>Cite "F.U.C.K. J. 2026" as a reference</li>
          <li>Be funny</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
      <div class="guideline">
        <h4>❌ DON'T</h4>
        <ul>
          <li>Be genuinely offensive (keep it silly)</li>
          <li>Submit actual serious analysis (wrong journal)</li>
          <li>Use AI-generated slop — write your own slop</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>

    </a>
    <p class="submit-note">(You will be redirected to GitHub. Don't panic.)</p>
  </div>
